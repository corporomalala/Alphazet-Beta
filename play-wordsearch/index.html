ALPHAZET is a product within the umbrella of CORPORO MALALA.

MADAM ELLA brought this into the world as a showcase of how AFRICA can and should leverage the 4th Industrial Revolution to leapgrog.

African languages are barely existant in the web. Just try Google-Translate for Lingala? A language in the central area of Africa, spoken by nearly 60 million people! The very beginning of a child's education starts with "ABC". And so, digital dominance must also start with language.

"Middle Easterns can easily browse the internet in arabic. Websites, softwares, etc -- tech is suited for them! Why not Africans? How do you expect the rural woman to take advantage of the benefits the internet offers, when she must first learn English (or French)? We must bring those languages into the internet and the metaverse!" -- MADAM ELLA

ALPHAZET is a solution to bring light to this crucial gap. INTO BINARY was more than happy to participate and code it all up.
